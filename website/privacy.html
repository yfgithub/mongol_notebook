Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Mongol Notebook Pro handles your information.

1. Data Collection

Mongol Notebook Pro is a privacy-first application. All notes, images, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The app may use system services (such as the Image Gallery or Share Sheet) to perform actions like saving images or sharing content. These interactions are handled entirely by your operating system's security framework.

3. Backups

The app provides a backup feature that allows you to export your data. These backup files are handled by you and can be stored in your preferred location (e.g., iCloud, local storage). We have no access to these backup files.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at yf_mst@163.com.
