Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I export my notes?
A: Open a note and tap the "Export" button in the sidebar. You can save it as a high-quality vertical image.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes, all notes are stored locally on your device. We recommend using the Backup feature to store your data safely in your personal cloud or local drive.
